About The Time Sphere

A global time intelligence platform built for a world without borders.

Our Mission

The world moves fast across time zones. Business calls, remote teams, international travel, global markets — knowing the exact local time somewhere in the world shouldn't require mental math or opening four apps. The Time Sphere gives you the answer in one click, beautifully.

What We Offer

Live clocks for every major city on Earth, updated every second. UTC offsets, DST status, IANA time zone names, and instant conversions to other time zones — all on one clean page. No account required. No noise. Just time.

Where We're Headed

The Time Sphere is growing into a full Global Time + Travel Intelligence Platform — with tools for meeting planning across time zones, luxury travel, charter flights, and concierge services for the world's most time-conscious professionals.
